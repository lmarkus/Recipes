# Strawberry Shortcake

## Ingredients

### Cake

* 100 g shortening
* 265 g granulated sugar
* 100 g (2) large eggs
* 14 g vanilla extract
* 4g salt
* 375 g all-purpose flour
* 13 g Baking Powder
* 245 g buttermilk / yoghurt

### Filling (Optional.  Can used whipped cream instead)
* Pastry Cream ([Link](https://github.com/lmarkus/Recipes/blob/main/Other/Pastry%20Cream.md))
* Srawberry jam/marmalade

### Strawberry Sauce
* Strawberries
* Sugar

### Whipped Cream Topping
** Note: Can sub with Reddi-Whip for ease **

* 450 g Heavy Cream
* 50 g granulated sugar

## Instructions

### 1- Strawberry Sauce
Start here, as this needs time. 

1. Chop/Slice Strawberries
2. Toss with Sugar
3. Let it macerate for an hour (Or longer) So that juices come out

### 2- Cake
Use manual electric beater for this one. It will give better results.

#### Preparation
1. Weight all ingredients beforehand. (Dry ingredients can be in the same container)
1. Preheat oven to 350F
2. Prepare Silicone Layer Cake Mold on top of FLAT tray.

#### Mixing
4. Sift Flour, Baking Powder, Salt
5. Warm Buttermilk 30 seconds in microwave. (Warm.  **NOT HOT**)
6. Cream Shortening + Sugar
7. Beat eggs separately. **Add about 1 egg at a time** into creamed shortening. Beat until it's creamy again before adding more.
8. Beat vanilal into cream
9. Add dry ingredients and warm buttermilk. Beat on slow/med until no dry ingredients / lumps remain.
10. Bake for 20 minutes
11. Let it cool down before unmolding. It's ok if the cake dries a little bit


### 3- Optional Filling
1. Beat pastry cream with Strawberry Jam

### 4- Whiped Cream
1. Mix Heavy Cream and Sugar
2. Whip with electric hand mixer to soft peaks.

## Assembly

1. Using each cup as a mold (Or a pastry ring), cut cake into smaller pieces that will be layered
2. Layer in this order:
a) Strawberry Syrup
b) Cake
c) Filling
d) Cake
e) Strawberry Syrup
f) Whipped Cream
g) Sliced Strawberries
h) Optional: Mint Garnish.

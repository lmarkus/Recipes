# Empanadas
Yield ~7 @ 145g Each

## Ingredients

* 375 g Harina Pan
* 75 g Flour
* 15 g Corn Starch
* 10 g Salt
* 20 g Sugar
* 520 g Warm Water

## Procedure

1. Mix flours and starch
2. Dissolve Sugar and Salt in warm water
3. Mix everithing together until smooth
4. Let it rest, covered, 5 minutes.
5. Divide, and prepare.
